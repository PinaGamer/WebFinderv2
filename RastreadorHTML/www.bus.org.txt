<p><span style="font-family:Times New Roman,Verdana;font-weight:bold;font-size:16pt;color:#0000ff"><center>The Path to</center></span>
<span style="font-family:Times New Roman,Verdana;font-weight:bold;font-size:16pt;color:#0000ff"><center>Serenity, Happiness and Salvation</center></span>
<p><span class="txt">
The Buddha imparted to us the relationship between delusion and the principle of causality.
He taught us the way of salvation, which releases us from the never-ending cycle of birth and death.
He told us that the path to serenity, happiness and salvation comes through a true understanding of life and nature.
Only through diligent cultivation, we can become truly liberated and enlightened.
</span>
